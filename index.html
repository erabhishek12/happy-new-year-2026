How do I create a personalized New Year wish?

Simply click on "Create Your Wish" button, choose a template, enter the recipient's name, customize it with your personal message and photo, then share or download!

Is this service completely free?

Yes! All features including template selection, customization, sharing, and downloading are 100% free. No hidden charges or subscriptions required.

Can I use my own photos?

Absolutely! You can upload your own photos (JPG, PNG, GIF formats) up to 5MB. Your photos make the wishes even more personal and special.

How can I share my created wishes?

You can share via WhatsApp, Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Telegram, Email, or copy the unique link. You can also download the image to share anywhere you like!

Can I create multiple wishes for different people?

Yes! Create unlimited personalized wishes for all your friends, family members, colleagues, and loved ones. Each wish gets its own unique link.

Will my personalized link expire?

No! Your personalized links are permanent. People can access them anytime, making it perfect for lasting memories.
